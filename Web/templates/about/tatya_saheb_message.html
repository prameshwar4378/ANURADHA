Karmayogi Tatya Saheb Bondre

The patriarch of the Anuradha Family


Message

       AEMS is a long cherished dream of Late Sri. Karmyogi Tatyasaheb Bondre, he directly elected by the public, served as the first Mayor of chikhli. He successfully managed a production of 92 tons from his sugarcane farm. Through the Anuradha family, Tatya Saheb Bondre initiated a new chapter in the educational, economic, social, spiritual, and health sectors, not just in Maharashtra but shining on the map of India. Like a seed planted in the vast expanse of the sky, he created a new history in the geography of district. In the face of adversity and lack of resources, he faced challenges head-on with determination, and even in difficult circumstances, he upheld culture and values. While serving as the Municipal President, he emphasized the importance of cleanliness and safe drinking water, propelling the development of the city of Renukanagari Chikhli. During his 28 visits to the Himalayas, including the Kailash-Manasarovar pilgrimage, and travels to Egypt's pyramids, the Vatican City, and Greek palaces, and exploration from Shakespeare to saintly literature, Tatya Saheb enriched his life with reading, contemplation, and discourse. His engagements included lectures and discussions, as well as exploration into the realms of pranayama, yoga, and exercise. He ventured into various fields, leaving no aspect untouched by his scholarly insights, from literary discussions to scientific debates.
