       ANURADHA ENGLISH MEDIUM SCHOOL, Chikhli, was established by Karmyogi Tatyasheb Bondre , under the aegis of the society of Paramhans Ramkrishna Maunibaba shikshan Sansthan, August 10th - 2003, with a commitment to pay his dues to Chikhli and a vision to provide holistic education with international standards with Indian values for the children of Chikhli and its surrounding areas.

       The school is situated in a 15 acre lush green campus on the outskirts of Chikhli , in a serene atmosphere, free from pollution, to enable the children to discover their true potential and acquire the requisite skills. The multi-storied school building provides all the modern amenities which include a multipurpose hall equipped with LCD and audio-video system, library, reading room, smart classrooms, indoor & outdoor sports facilities, science labs, mathematics lab, RO water purifier with cooler, hygienic toilets for boys and girls in each floor.

       The School is day boarding co-ed school from Grade 1st to Grade 10th . We are affiliated to the CBSE Delhi vide affiliation No. 1130855. Message

       The purpose of education is to ignite a spirit of inquiry, creativity, a thirst for knowledge, striving for excellence, character-building, self-development as well as cultivate good habits to last a life-time. Anuradha English Medium School, Chikhli strives tirelessly to mentor its students to realize their potential and develop confidence to face the future challenges in a fiercely competitive world moving at an accelerated pace.

       Every child is like a seed which has the potential to grow if it is nourished with love and care under the right guidance. ANURADHA ENGLISH MEDIUM SCHOOL, Chikhli is a child friendly school where each and every child is understood and valued. Personal attention is given to the development of children in all spheres; academics, sports and cultural.

       It is our constant endeavor to groom our children for life; making those well - rounded individuals who will be able to contribute to the society they live in, who shall set out to leave the world a better place than what they find it to be. We are always open for new ideas and suggestions which can add more value.
